About Luis

Photography & Lifestyle

 

 

Hey there! My name is Luis Abate , I am a full time photographer, social media strategist, and a part time lifestyle/travel blogger. I am based in my hometown Tegucigalpa, Honduras.

With only 22 years I have been blessed with many great life experiences. My passion for photography and social media has taken me places I never even dreamt when it all started back in 2011.


I describe myself as a very driven person, also a perfectionist and a hard worker. Belive it or not I am very sensitive, which leads me to be compassionate, kind, and very down to earth. I look forward to working with you!
